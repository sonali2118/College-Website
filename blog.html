Our Certificates & Online Programs For 2024

Accredited online certificate programs allow students to prepare for their desired career without pursuing a traditional 4-year degree. Many colleges and universities offer certificate programs online, allowing flexibility to complete coursework from anywhere. Learners can earn certificates in fields such as education, healthcare, business, and technology. Schools also offer certificates in trades such as plumbing and welding.


Many trade jobs offer above-average salaries. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), construction workers earn a median annual salary of $62,860. Plumbers and pipefitters earn $56,330, while HVAC professionals earn $50,590. These figures exceed the median annual salary of $41,950 for all occupations.


Students should only apply to accredited online certificate programs. Accreditation helps ensure that higher education institutions meet acceptable quality standards. Schools may feature regional or national accreditation. Individual departments or programs may feature programmatic accreditation.


Online certificate programs prepare students for careers in many fields. Most programs focus on one area of study, such as law enforcement or accounting.
